Biography

Experience That Connects the Classroom to the Field

Dr. Timothy A. Whitley is the founder and owner of Whitley Enterprise LLC, an audio visual engineer, technical director, educator, author, minister, and United States military veteran. His professional background spans live sound, broadcast, studio production, AV systems, electrical and installation work, networking, technical training, and ministry leadership.

His writing reflects a practical teaching philosophy: complex systems become understandable when foundational principles are connected to real-world decisions. The Whitley Audio Visual Engineering Handbook was created to preserve that knowledge and equip technicians, students, educators, churches, and professionals for lasting excellence.

“Knowledge becomes powerful when it can be understood, applied, taught, and passed forward.”
